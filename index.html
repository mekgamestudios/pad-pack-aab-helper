FORGOTTEN QUEST

In this single-player fantasy RPG, harness the power of three different classes in one character! Freely switch between sword mastery, archery, and magic to create your own unique combat style. Gain strategic advantages by using each class’s unique abilities and defeat your enemies with skill and strategy.

  • ✔️ Top-down gameplay with intuitive controls

  • ✔️ Play offline anytime you want
